The Coven's Walls
Gather Materials
Garradia has asked you to gather some materials to prepare for the Coven's new protective ritual. She requires Venomgaze Bile from Venomgaze Basilisks, a Horn from a Leafmane Alpha, and an Ugdenbloom found growing around the bog or from the Ugdenbog Golems.{^n}{^n}When you have all three, bring them to Garradia at the Coven's Refuge in Ugdenbog in exchange for iron bits.
Gather Venomgaze Bile
Gather Leafmane Alpha Horn
Gather Ugdenbloom
Bring the materials to Garradia at the Coven's Refuge
