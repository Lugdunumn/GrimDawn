You were assaulted by a Chthonian monstrosity, perhaps the result of a failed summoning.{^n}{^n}Upon closer inspection, these Bloodsworn seem familiar. Search the Temple of Ateph, in the Ruins of Abyd north of the Vanguard of the Three, for any clues regarding how these cultists got here and what they were trying to do.
